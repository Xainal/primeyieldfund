FAQ

Common questions from global holders.

What is the softcap? Is it liquidity?

Softcap is the minimum amount of SOL required for a successful presale. Softcap is not liquidity. Liquidity comes from a portion of presale funds + project tokens.

Are profits or returns guaranteed?

No. PYF does not guarantee any profit, yield, or fixed return. Crypto is high risk.

How will presale funds be used?

For liquidity, marketing, development, and productive business expansion. No revenue or ROI promises are allowed.
